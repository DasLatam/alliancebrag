Cookies Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device while you browse the web. They store information about your preferences and help improve your experience on our site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies: essential for the site to work and cannot be disabled.
  • Performance cookies: collect anonymous data about how visitors interact with our site.
  • Functionality cookies: personalize your experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Advertising cookies: show you content and ads relevant to your interests.

3. Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you control or disable cookies through their settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect how parts of our site function.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy at any time. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
