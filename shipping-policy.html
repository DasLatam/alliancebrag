Shipping Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Geographic Coverage

We ship to every United States territory.

2. Delivery Times

Shipments take between 3 and 5 business days to be processed and delivered after an order is placed.

Shipping rates depend on package size and the shipping option selected at checkout — standard or expedited.
